**Example Reddit Post Title:** *The math behind why I kept failing prop firm challenges (and the exact risk calculator I built to fix it)*

1. Tell a story about blowing an account because you over-leveraged on NQ.
2. Break down the math of why standard position sizing is required.
3. Explain the formula you use to calculate risk.
4. At the very end, say: *"I got tired of doing this math on a napkin, so I built a free web app to do it instantly. If anyone wants to use it, it's at [Link]. No signup required, I just wanted to share."*
