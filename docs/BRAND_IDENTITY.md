# TraderStat: Anonymous Brand Identity & Social Profiles

To launch TraderStat successfully while remaining completely anonymous, we need to build a brand that speaks for itself. The brand must sound professional, trustworthy, and built by a real trader who understands the pain points of the market.

## 1. Core Brand Identity

### Brand Name
**TraderStat**
(Alternative backups if domains/handles are taken: RiskForge, EdgeMetrics, PropEdge).

### Tagline
*Stop Trading Blind. Start Trading Smart.*
(Secondary: *The math behind your trading edge.*)

### Colour Scheme
The brand uses a modern, "dark mode" financial aesthetic. It signals professionalism and reduces eye strain for traders who stare at screens all day.
- **Primary Accent**: Teal-Green (`#00D4AA`) — Represents profit, "green days," and forward momentum.
- **Background**: Deep Space / Navy (`#0D1117` to `#1A1A2E`) — Professional dark mode.
- **Warning/Loss**: Red-Pink (`#E94560`) — Used sparingly for drawdown alerts and stop losses.

### Logo Concept
Since you are bootstrapping, use a clean, text-based logo with an emoji or simple icon.
- **Icon**: 📊 (Bar Chart) or 🧮 (Abacus/Calculator).
- **Font**: Inter (Weight: 800/ExtraBold).
- **Execution**: Simply write "TraderStat" in white, with the "Stat" portion in the Primary Teal-Green (`#00D4AA`). Use Canva or Figma to export this as a transparent PNG.

---

## 2. The "Anonymous Founder" Persona

You will operate under a pseudonym or simply as "The TraderStat Team." 
**Persona**: A quantitative-leaning retail trader who got tired of blowing funded accounts due to poor risk management and decided to build the tools they needed to survive.

**Tone of Voice**: Direct, analytical, no-nonsense. You do not promise lambos or overnight millions. You talk about risk management, R-multiples, drawdowns, and surviving the market. This builds massive trust.
