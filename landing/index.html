FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is TraderStat really free?
Yes. All three tools — Trade Journal, Risk Calculator, and Prop Firm Tracker — are completely free to use. No credit card, no signup, no subscription. Just open the app and start using it.
Do I need to create an account?
No. You can use TraderStat immediately without creating an account. Your data is stored locally in the app session. For persistent data across sessions, a free account option is coming soon.
What instruments does the Risk Calculator support?
The Risk Calculator supports Stocks (fractional shares), Futures (whole contracts — ES, NQ, MES, MNQ, CL, GC, etc.), and Options (premium-based sizing). The calculator automatically adjusts rounding based on the instrument type you select.
Which prop firms does the Prop Firm Tracker support?
The tracker works with any prop firm. You manually enter your account rules (max drawdown, daily loss limit, profit target) and the app tracks your progress. It includes a built-in reference table for FTMO, Apex Trader Funding, TopStep, My Forex Funds, and The Funded Trader.
What is an R multiple?
R (Risk) is the dollar amount you risk on a trade. An R multiple tells you how many times your initial risk you made or lost. A +2R trade means you made twice your risk. A -1R trade means you lost your full risk amount. Tracking R multiples lets you evaluate performance independently of account size or position size.
Does TraderStat connect to my broker?
Not currently. TraderStat is a manual trade logging tool. You enter your trades after they're executed. This keeps the app simple, fast, and broker-agnostic — it works with any broker worldwide.
Will this always be free?
The core tools will always have a free tier. In the future, advanced features like unlimited trade history and enhanced analytics may require a paid subscription. Early users will always receive the best pricing.
